A LETTER OF APPRECIATION TO THE ALL THE INSTRUCTORS

Dear instructors,

This letter is a sincere token of gratitude to teachers. Those who guide us through some of the most chaotic times of our lives.
We want to remind you how much of an impact you can have, not just on each student you teach, but as a profession and a community beyond the four walls of a classroom. Enjoy this collection of thank you notes from students to teachers who were integral in course of their lives.

Thank you for your love, care, admonishment and disciplined culture that you taught me. It really went a long way in defining me today.Now i can produly say that i have the following skills:

  • Transformational leadership
  • Project management.
  • Design thinking.

I had so many dreams, and I don’t think I could have accomplished any of them without your example. You changed the way I thought about life and about learning. Now I know that I’m capable of anything with enough dedication and hard work. Once again thank you for being our guide and beacon of hope.

Thank you!


Yours sincerly,

Judith Ugbo.
